Write SEO-optimized content for: $ARGUMENTS

## Instructions

1. **Keyword Research** — identify primary + 5 LSI keywords
2. **Title Tag** — SEO title (under 60 chars, include main keyword)
3. **Meta Description** — (under 155 chars, include keyword + CTA)
4. **Content Structure:**
   - H1 (main title)
   - Introduction with keyword in first 100 words
   - H2/H3 subheadings with keywords
   - Body content (informative, engaging)
   - FAQ section (targets featured snippets)
   - Conclusion with CTA
5. **Internal linking suggestions**
6. **SEO Score checklist**

## Output Format:

### Primary Keyword:
[keyword]

### LSI Keywords:
[list of 5 related keywords]

### Title Tag (SEO):
[title - max 60 chars]

### Meta Description:
[description - max 155 chars]

### Full Article:
[complete article with H1, H2, H3 structure]

### FAQ Section:
Q: [question]
A: [answer]
(5 FAQs minimum)

### SEO Checklist:
- [ ] Keyword in title
- [ ] Keyword in first paragraph
- [ ] Keyword in at least one H2
- [ ] Images with alt text suggested
- [ ] Internal links suggested
- [ ] External authority links suggested

## Rules
- Keyword density: 1-2% (natural, not stuffed)
- Write for HUMANS first, search engines second
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Include data, statistics, examples
- Answer the search intent completely
